Unity Project: Corales

This was one of my more creative and challenging projects because it was very tedious and very difficult to get the shapes,points,circles,and lines where I wanted them to go. In this assignment I was asked to use the skills I learned from my previous CFU'S and combine them together to make a project where it had shapes,points,circles and lines. The theme was making a holiday card inspired by any holiday of our choosing. I chose to make a christmas holiday card where I wanted to incoorperate a snowy day using canva.draw_.

Skills:For loop,canvas.draw_line, canvas.draw_point, canvas.draw_polygon, canvas.draw_circle, frame.set_draw_handler
