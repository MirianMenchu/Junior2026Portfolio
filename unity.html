Unity Project: Corales

This was a 2D project for G4C on the topic Reef and Forest. My goal was to create a game where the player is a small character who collects compost,water,trees, and corals. The purpose of my game was to spread awareness on the issue of the damages we as a society cause to our environment. I showed this through my character, while it is a good message I also wanted to include a challenge where the character faces obstacles that can kill them. This creates an illusion that while we may try and help our environment there may be obstacles that we may face that will stop us from truly helping the animals and the animals environment. Can you beat this game?

Skills:Restart system, Health and score system, jumping mechanics, Left/right movements , Fully working player controller

Figma: Infy App Prototype

I was introduced to Figma this year and this was one of the most challenging but exciting thing I have done this year. I was able to create something not just out of my mind but also the mind of my fellow peers/teamates. Our task was to create an app that addressed an issue we think should be addressed. The issue we chose was mental health but mostly directed towards teens. We believed this should be talked about more which is why we created an app to help lower stress and find ways to cope in a healthier manner. It was a challenge for all us but in the end our hard work formed something we were very proud of towards the end. So come check it out!

Skills: Teamwork, Shapes, Prototypes, Collaboration, Ideas,

Figma: Portfolio
